Pre-Adoption Checklist

Please verify each requirement before proceeding with the exotic adoption process.

Local and State Legality

Confirmed species legality in state, city, and neighborhood. (This includes your landlord/HOA Board)

Specialized Veterinary Care

Find a certified veterinarian for the exotic animal you plan to adopt within a 50 mile radius. Make sure they are actively accepting new patients and can provide care for your animal.

Emergency Power Plan

Backup plan for when electricity goes out. You must have a back up generator to maintain climate control during outages.

Specialized Diet Source

Establish a reliable way/source of getting nutrient-dense foods for your exotic pet.

Life Cycle Commitment

Plan and prepare for the full lifespan of your exotic pet.
